🚀 How to deploy on Render.com

  1. Go to your Render dashboard → open your NovaSpark service
  2. Click Environment in the left sidebar
  3. Find the SESSION_ID variable and paste your session string above
  4. Click Save Changes and then Manual Deploy
  5. Your bot should come online within ~30 seconds ⚡
